QUESTION:Explain the disparity between closing tags and self-closing tags in HTML?

ANSWER: The major disparity between a self closing tag and a closing tag in HTML as its conoted by the name is that self-closing HTML tag does not need to be closed manually by its closing tag,while a closing tag has a separate closing tag for itself. Here are some examples of self-closing and closing tags;

Block Elements: Inline Elements:
